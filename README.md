# Squid Game

**Squid Game** is a South Korean survival drama series created by Hwang Dong-hyuk. It became a global phenomenon after its release on Netflix in 2021.

## Main Characters

- Seong Gi-hun (Player 456)
- Cho Sang-woo (Player 218)
- Kang Sae-byeok (Player 067)
- Abdul Ali (Player 199)
- The Front Man
- Hwang Jun-ho

## Plot Summary

> Hundreds of cash-strapped contestants accept an invitation to compete in children's games for a tempting prize. However, the stakes are deadly, and only one can survive.

## Why I Like It

- The storyline is intense and unpredictable.  
- It explores themes like inequality, desperation, and human nature.  
- Strong acting and cinematography.

## Favorite Quote

> "You have a reason to get out of here, but I don’t."
